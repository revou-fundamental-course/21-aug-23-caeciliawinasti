Hi Guest, Welcome To RevoU Boarding School

RevoU Boarding School is one of the best Boarding School in the world. Located deep down in the middle of the Pacific Ocean, this school can only be reached by boats or in some case, plane, too. This school accept all kind of students as they willing to live in the dormitories until they graduate.

Living in dormitories has its own advantages. Students can get more opportunities to make friends because living in the same conditions can bring people closer. By living closer to schools, Teachers can also supervise students better. Students will have healthier livestyle too with our scheduled activity.

Vision Mission
Ensure that all students live in the same environment, so as to obtain maximum learning outcomes Become the best boarding school where students can come to learn not only from school, but also learn how to live in a community so they can prepare themselves to live in society
